Step 1 · Go

Go to Watermark Coffee Shop.

When you arrive, pause before going inside. Ask the Lord for eyes to see what He wants to reveal through this trail.

Location Watermark Coffee Shop Prototype location for Gold Vein V1

Step 2 · Connect

Show your Gold Vein code.

Go to the counter and order a coffee. When you are ready, show the QR code below. The code proves you are on this adventure and redeems one coffee from the prepaid Gold Vein gift-card balance.

Watermark Redemption Code GV-WM-NO1-001 Adventure No. 1 · One coffee · Gift-card balance

Step 3 · Small Treasure

Receive the gift.

You received your coffee as a gift, a tangible treasure for this part of the trail. Let it remind you that small gifts can point toward deeper things. Receive it with gratitude, then keep going. The bigger treasure is still ahead.

Step 4 · Map

Open 2 Peter 1:3-11.

  • Read 2 Peter 1:3-11 and underline what the passage says about entrance into the Kingdom of Christ.
  • Notice what Peter connects to entrance into the eternal Kingdom.
  • Be all the more diligent to make certain about His calling and choosing you; for as long as you practice these things, you will never stumble.
  • Invite fellow brothers and sisters in Christ on your adventure, talk to them about this passage of Scripture, and experience the abundance of this big treasure.

Step 5 · Big Treasure

Name what was uncovered.

The big treasure is not the coffee. It is what the Lord reveals through His Word, His people, and your step of obedience. Record what happened, share the testimony, and carry the treasure forward.

Project Structure

Gold Vein Framework

Each trail begins with one clear observation and grows from there. The first step should be small, focused, and possible to complete.

01

Observation

One phrase, verse, question, need, or moment that catches the participant's attention.

02

Map

A focused Scripture passage or biblical theme that gives the observation its trail.

03

Little Adventure

A short mission of faith, conversation, action, or service that can actually be done.

04

Companionship

Including at least one other person through presence, conversation, prayer, or witness.

05

Commentary

Biblical and personal reflection on what the experience revealed.

06

The Treasure

The deeper truth and tangible evidence discovered, received, recorded, and acted on.
