Core Loop

Receive. Go. Connect. Obey. Reflect. Testify.

01

Receive

A letter, link, QR code, invitation, or direct assignment begins the trail.

02

Go

The participant moves toward a person, place, question, act, or Scripture passage.

03

Connect

The adventure includes companionship through conversation, presence, or shared mission.

04

Obey

The participant takes a small faithful step instead of only consuming content.

05

Reflect

Commentary and Field Notes help name what the experience revealed.

06

Testify

The treasure is carried back as truth, evidence, gratitude, or encouragement.

Framework

Disciple-making by adventure.

Gold Vein is built around Spirit-led trails where Scripture gives light, obedience takes shape, people are connected, and Jesus Christ is discovered, received, carried, and given as the treasure.

01

Unbeliever

A trail can invite someone to come and look to Christ through Scripture, honest questions, embodied kindness, and witness toward new birth.

02

Believer

A trail can help a believer stuck in sin, fear, or addiction move from isolation into confession, Scripture, companionship, obedience, and freedom.

03

Disciple Maker

A trail can send a follower of Jesus to pray, serve, teach, follow up, and help another person take the next faithful step.

04

Unlocks

Each step unlocks through a real act: place, pass, Word, person, gift, or witness. The app serves the obedience.

05

Scripture Map

The Word is not decoration. It gives the trail light through context, history, inner life, Spirit-led attention, power, and fruit.

06

The Treasure

The Big Treasure is Christ revealed in power, not only in words. Along the way, travelers may also receive real-world treasures: prepared gifts such as coffee, resources, merch, financial help, or other tangible signs of grace.
