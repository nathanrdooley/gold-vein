Project Structure

Gold Vein Framework

Each trail begins with one clear observation and grows from there. The first step should be small, focused, and possible to complete.

01

Observation

One phrase, verse, question, need, or moment that catches the participant's attention.

02

Map

A focused Scripture passage or biblical theme that gives the observation its trail.

03

Little Adventure

A short mission of faith, conversation, action, or service that can actually be done.

04

Companionship

Including at least one other person through presence, conversation, prayer, or witness.

05

Commentary

Biblical and personal reflection on what the experience revealed.

06

The Treasure

The deeper truth and tangible evidence discovered, received, recorded, and acted on.
