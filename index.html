About

What Gold Vein is about.

Gold Vein is a Spirit-led, Scripture-supported adventure app for disciple-making. It helps people take small steps of obedience into real spaces, real conversations, and real moments where Jesus Christ is revealed as the treasure.

01

Little Adventures

Each trail gives a simple step to take: go, look, receive, connect, respond, serve, or follow up.

02

Scripture Maps

The Word gives light to the trail through context, truth, inner attention, obedience, and power.

03

Real-World Treasures

Some trails include tangible gifts such as coffee, resources, financial help, or prepared tokens of grace.

04

The Big Treasure

The Big Treasure is Christ revealed and carried outward through faith, repentance, love, mercy, testimony, and disciple-making.

Step 3 · Look

Experience the passage.

Instruction: Read 2 Peter 1:3-11.

Attention: What does Peter say is given? What does he tell us to practice? What does he say about entrance into the Kingdom?

Open Scripture Map

If you have a Bible with you, open it first. Use this map to slow down and discover the passage with history, culture, inner attention, and the power of God by the Spirit.

Context

Peter writes to believers who need endurance, clarity, and confidence as they follow Christ in a costly world.

History + Culture

Notice the language of calling, choosing, virtue, knowledge, kingdom, and entrance. Peter is forming a people who live as citizens of Christ's Kingdom.

Inner Life

Pay attention to desire, fear, diligence, assurance, and stumbling. What does this passage expose, strengthen, or heal in you?

Spirit-Led Attention

Ask the Lord to give light. What word, phrase, command, promise, or conviction is being made alive as you read?

Power + Fruit

Trace what God has given, what Peter says to practice, and what kind of entrance is supplied into the eternal Kingdom of Jesus Christ.

Step 1 · Look Begin with honesty before God.

Step 1 · Look

Begin with honesty.

Instruction: Pause and ask: "Jesus, if You are the Son of God, help me see what is true."

Attention: Notice what rises in you: curiosity, resistance, fear, hope, confusion, desire, or weariness.

This app cannot convert anyone. It can only help you come into the light and look to Christ.

Honest willingness is required before continuing.

Step 2 · Hear

Receive the gospel invitation.

Instruction: Hear the good news: God loved the world and gave His Son, that whoever believes in Him shall not perish but have eternal life.

Attention: Do not rush past the word gave. Salvation is received by faith, not earned by performance.

Gospel Invitation GV-GSP-CONV-001 Look to Christ · Believe · Receive life

Receive the gospel invitation to unlock the Scripture Map.

Step 3 · Word

Look at John 3:1-18.

Instruction: Read John 3:1-18 slowly.

Attention: What does Jesus say must happen? What does God give? What does belief receive? What does light expose?

Open Conversion Scripture Map

If you have a Bible with you, open it first. Use this map to discover the conversation, promise, warning, invitation, and power of the passage.

Context

Nicodemus is religious, respected, and still needs new birth from above.

Need

Jesus says a person must be born again to see and enter the kingdom of God.

Gift

The Father gives the Son. The Son is lifted up. Eternal life is received by believing in Him.

Inner Life

Notice whether you are hiding from light, coming toward light, or asking for mercy.

Power + Fruit

The Big Treasure is Christ revealed as Savior and Lord, bringing life where there was death.

Step 4 · Respond

Name your honest response.

Instruction: Choose the response that is most honest right now, then write one sentence.

Attention: The goal is truth in the light, not pretending to be somewhere you are not.

Choose your honest response.

Honest response confirmation required before continuing.

Step 5 · Follow

Connect for follow-up.

Instruction: Invite a follower of Jesus into this moment for prayer, questions, confession, baptism, church, or the next faithful step.

Attention: New life is not meant to be carried alone. Follow Christ with His people.

"I am on a Gold Vein Conversion Trail. I read John 3 and need someone to pray with me and help me take the next step."

Follow-up connection required before this trail can be completed.

Framework

Disciple-making by adventure.

Gold Vein is built around Spirit-led trails where Scripture gives light, obedience takes shape, people are connected, and Jesus Christ is discovered, received, carried, and given as the treasure.

01

Unbeliever

A trail can invite someone to come and look to Christ through Scripture, honest questions, embodied kindness, and witness toward new birth.

02

Believer

A trail can help a believer stuck in sin, fear, or addiction move from isolation into confession, Scripture, companionship, obedience, and freedom.

03

Disciple Maker

A trail can send a follower of Jesus to pray, serve, teach, follow up, and help another person take the next faithful step.

04

Unlocks

Each step unlocks through a real act: place, pass, Word, person, gift, or witness. The app serves the obedience.

05

Scripture Map

The Word is not decoration. It gives the trail light through context, history, inner life, Spirit-led attention, power, and fruit.

06

The Treasure

The Big Treasure is Christ revealed in power, not only in words. Along the way, travelers may also receive real-world treasures: prepared gifts such as coffee, resources, merch, financial help, or other tangible signs of grace.
